About Mina

Myoui Mina (名井 南), known simply as Mina, is a Japanese singer and dancer based in South Korea. She is one of the main dancers and sub vocalists of the South Korean girl group TWICE under JYP Entertainment. She was born on March 24, 1997, in San Antonio, Texas, USA, and she grew up in Japan.
